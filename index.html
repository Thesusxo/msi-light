About M.S.I Light

Your Trusted Partner in
Electrical Trading

M.S.I Light Enterprise is a registered sole proprietorship specializing in general trading, dealing in electrical appliances and phone accessories, and the import and export of general goods. Based in Lashibi, Accra — Greater Accra Region, Ghana — we serve as a reliable bridge between international manufacturers and local markets.

Our operations span retail sale in non-specialized stores (ISIC 4719) as well as the retail sale of electrical household appliances, furniture, lighting equipment, and other household articles in specialized stores (ISIC 4759). We supply quality general goods to businesses and consumers across the region.
